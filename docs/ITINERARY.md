### Ba Na Hills tips
- Beli tiket online malam sebelumnya (KKday / Klook / Sun World website)
- Masuk jam 08:00 — langsung ke **Golden Bridge** sebelum ramai
- Bawa jaket tipis — suhu di atas ~17–20°C vs 30°C di bawah
- Bawa snack dari luar — makanan di dalam mahal
- Fog pagi hari normal — kabut biasanya hilang jam 10–11
- Buffet lunch ~Rp 130rb extra — bisa bawa dari luar

## Important notes

- **Tan Dinh Church (Gereja Pink)** — under renovation per 2026, exterior only
- **Dragon Bridge fire show** — only Friday, Saturday, Sunday at 21:00 WIB
- **Notre-Dame Cathedral** — under renovation, exterior photos only
- **Ba Na Hills July–August** — peak season (school holidays), arrive before 09:00 for best experience
- **Tan Son Nhat Airport HCM** — traffic very unpredictable in the morning, leave hotel by 06:00 for a 09:35 flight
- **War Remnants Museum** — allocate minimum 2 hours, emotionally heavy
